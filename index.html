About Me

Manufacturing IT Specialist & Practical Problem Solver

I bridge the gap between factory floor and business systems. For 13+ years at Toyota Boshoku, I've built reliable VB.NET applications that actually get used in production—PLC integration systems that pull real-time data from the factory floor, automated reporting solutions that transformed hours of manual work into minutes, and bulletproof 24/7 systems that keep production running.

My specialty: taking messy production data and turning it into actionable insights. I understand both the technical side (VB.NET, SQL Server, industrial protocols) and the operational side (manufacturing processes, quality tracking, what operators actually need). This hybrid perspective lets me build solutions that are practical, maintainable, and solve real problems.
